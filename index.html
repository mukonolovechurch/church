WHO WE ARE
Mukono Love Church is a global Born-Again Pentecostal Church dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Founded in some early years, it has since grown to over 50 church branches worldwide, impacting millions of lives. Mukono Love Church is committed to holistic community development through church planting, global missions, leadership training, and social work.

As a church birthed out of evangelism our work goes beyond the church building to our immediate community and the entire nation at large. We encourage you to come and fellowship with us as we aim to serve God. We also operate five radio stations and a television station.

Mukono Love Church is a beacon of hope and inspiration, demonstrating the transformative power of JESUS CHRIST.
